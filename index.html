Canada's Greatest University

While there are many prestigious postsecondary academic institutions in Canada, including the likes of McGill, the University of Toronto, and the University of British Columbia, one stands out above the rest in its international reach, size, and impact on Canadian society: l'Université de Hearst. 

The behemoth Northern Ontario univesity has three separate campuses in Hearst, Timmins, and Kapuskasing. It employs 30 academic staff and includes a combined 280 students (approximately 63% of the capacity of Carleton's largest lecture hall).

L'Université de Hearst is one of Ontario's only fully francophone universities and provides a local postsecondary option for high school graduates in Northern Ontario. While it has been in operation since 1953, it has only been fully independent from Laurentian University since April 1, 2022. Students can study in multiple Liberal Arts fields, including psychology. In order to facilitate learning for student coming from outside the metropolitan areas of Timmins, Kapuskasing, and Hearst, all three campuses have student accomodation on campus.
